Millet Vegetable Khichdi

A wholesome one-pot meal using our organic foxtail millet.

Prep: 10 mins
Cook: 25 mins
Serves: 3
lunchmilletshealthy

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Firoz Farms foxtail millet
  • 1/2 cup moong dal
  • Mixed vegetables
  • 1 tbsp ghee
  • Spices to taste

Steps

  1. 1

    Wash millet and dal together.

  2. 2

    Sauté vegetables and spices in ghee.

  3. 3

    Add millet, dal, and water; pressure cook until soft.

  4. 4

    Serve hot with a dollop of ghee.
